how it started- history

From the get-go, the intentions were the same: have a space for students to come and be themselves; unwind. It never really matriculated into that, though. This facility quickly became an unoccupied, empty room. It would be swept and kept clean but that was about it. There was never really anyone around to tend to until one day late 2022. A student after their numerous trips pass this room, one day thought it would be a good idea to find out what its initial purpose was for. To the students surprise,it was a vacant space meant for students. A team was formed and let's just say what we have as a full-fledge lounge today, is a direct product of the teams consistent efforts.

how it's going-present day
